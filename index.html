About Us

Activ8 is a digital lifestyle experiential agency

We create engaging experiences

The growth of a brand depends on its ability to engage its target audience. We work with brands to accelerate their growth through experiential activations. The process involves setting up engagement channels within an environment that supports the exchange between brand and consumer.

Furthermore, we are able to measure and analyze consumer feedback to provide the brand with leads and directives that guarantee conversion.

Event Management & Production

Crafting and managing proceedings of Events, showcases, exhibitions and other brand activations.

Attendee Engagement Reporting

Feedback and reaction direct from the attendees and other consumers present at a brand’s activation.

Live coverage and broadcasting

Brand experiences shared via live stream to reach a larger audience to boost brand visibility.
